Case Study – Restaurant Digital Transformation Scenario

As the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of a local restaurant, your mission is to lead the digital transformation by developing a web-based solution that enhances customer experience and operational efficiency. The goal is to provide customers with an elegant, user-friendly platform to explore the menu, make bookings, and send enquiries — all integrated into a single digital ecosystem.

Problem Context

Many small restaurants struggle to keep up with digital expectations. Customers expect the convenience of online reservations, contactless menus, and instant communication channels. This project demonstrates how technology can solve these challenges through a simple yet powerful web application prototype.

Proposed Solution: Web Application Features

  • Dynamic Menu: Displays dishes, descriptions, and prices that can be easily updated by staff.
  • Online Booking System: Customers can reserve a table with their preferred date and time.
  • Customer Enquiries: A simple form for feedback, catering requests, or special events.
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
