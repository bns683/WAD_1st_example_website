Case Study Overview

As the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of a local restaurant, my objective was to support the restaurant’s digital transformation by designing and developing a web-based solution that enhances customer engagement, modernizes communication, and streamlines the table booking process.

The restaurant had been relying on manual phone bookings and walk-in management, which led to inefficiencies, limited marketing reach, and missed opportunities for customer feedback. To address these challenges, I led the design of an interactive online platform — a central digital hub where customers can book tables, send feedback, and connect via social media.

Business Goals

  • Improve customer engagement and loyalty through digital channels.
  • Enable seamless online table reservations and customer feedback collection.
  • Integrate the restaurant’s web presence with major social media platforms.
  • Provide customers with quick access to contact information and promotions.

Design Concept & Rationale

The page design prioritizes clarity, usability, and real-world functionality. By keeping the layout clean and the form interaction simple, the solution provides a tangible demonstration of how digital technologies can modernize traditional restaurant operations. The “mailto” integration enables instant communication through the user’s email client — a lightweight yet effective method for small businesses transitioning into digital systems.

This design concept mirrors the broader philosophy of digital transformation strategy — using accessible, scalable web tools to deliver measurable value quickly, while laying a foundation for future upgrades such as real-time booking systems, CRM integration, and analytics dashboards.
